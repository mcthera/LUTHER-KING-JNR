DO YOU KNOW MARTHIN LUTHER KING JNR?

Mr. Luther King Kwame Adinkra Junior

Popularly called Kwame Adinkra, he is a Ghanaian broadcast journalist, singer, philanthropist, and public speaker. He was formerly the host and chief operating officer manager for Angel Broadcasting Network.

He is the Morning Show Host on Pure FM and currently lectures at one of Ghana’s leading universities, USTED in Winneba.

Education & Career

He holds a Master of Philosophy in communication design from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, and a Bachelor of Arts degree in graphic design from the same university.

The Tradition Trademark Value

He respects tradition and culture with strong roots at the Manhyia Palace. He is a member of the five-person planning committee formed by OTUMFUO OSEI TUTU II, King of ASANTE, to support the Otumfuo Charity Foundation Teachers Award.

He has featured as a judge on TV shows such as TV3 Mentor, Ghana's Most Beautiful, and Coca-Cola Hit Single. He also chaired the entertainment committee for the Kumasi Venue Organizing Committee of CAN 2008.

His influence continues to shape modern presentation, and his impact will last forever.
